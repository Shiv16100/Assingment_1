
Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence refers to the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems.
Specific applications of AI include expert systems, natural language processing (NLP), speech recognition, and machine vision.

Major Subfields:
1. Machine Learning
2. Deep Learning
3. Reinforcement Learning
4. Computer Vision
5. Robotics

Applications:
- Healthcare: Diagnosing diseases using AI-powered models.
- Finance: Fraud detection and risk management.
- Transportation: Autonomous vehicles.
- Education: Personalized learning systems.

Challenges:
- Data privacy and bias.
- Lack of transparency in decision-making (Black-box models).
- Ethical and employment concerns.

AI continues to revolutionize industries by increasing efficiency and enabling innovation.
